Hi I'm

Geraldine d'Epenoux

I'm passionate about challenging projects that allow me to explore my creativity and knowledge. I'm looking to work in an environment that will allow me to grow, show my potential and have an impact onto the world.

My background is mainly film based, having studied theatre and film in London. I also like to experiment with photography, intervening them to create new moods and feelings. The power of visuals and what they can express drive me in my work.

I studied UX/UI design at Ironhack where I got to improve my skills, develop new ones and take projects all the way form concept to working prototypes. There I discovered a new passion for creating user consious designs that can not only help in the overall experience but, hopefully, make a small but meaningful change in their lives. I like focusing my projects on the human aspect of UX/UI design.
